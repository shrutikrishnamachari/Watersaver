TIPS

Take shorter showers! Shortening your showers by just a few minutes can save thousands of dollars a year!

Switch to high-effinciency appliances! It can save you 7,000 gallons a year!

When washing dishes or brushing your teeth, don't leave your water running!

Only use the dish washer or washing machine when they are full. This can save you over 10,000 gallons a year!

Frequently check for leaks, and when you find them, fix them! Leaks can attribute to the loss of anywhere from 2,000 to 20,0000 gallons of water a year!

Use a broom to clean instead of a hose, and make sure your hose is turned off completely! This can save over 10,000 gallons
